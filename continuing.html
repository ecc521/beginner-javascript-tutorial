Here are some tips to help you continue on with JavaScript:


When trying to figure out how to do something, Stack Overflow and Mozilla Developer Network are extremely valuable assets.

Stack Overflow contains answers for almost any question you could possibly think of, and Mozilla Developer Network has excellent documentation and examples most things web related.

You may also find W3 Schools helpful for it's examples, though the advertisements can be very distracting.

Most of all, look around. Type your question into your favorite search engine, and something should come up.
